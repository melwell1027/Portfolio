Hello,

a bit about me:

My name is Matthew Elwell and I am an Apple Genius, aspiring web developer, and customer service extraordinaire.

I have always had a passion for technology and chose to make it my career. With over five years experience with Apple, I have had the joy of immersing myself in this world. From customer support, to sales, to hardware repair and software troubleshooting, I have gained a well-rounded understanding of what it takes to make a customer and user experience great.

With a drive to develop further, I recently completed a Full-Stack Coding Bootcamp at Northwestern University. The course focused on the MERN Stack (MongoDB/MySQL, Express, React, and Node). I loved designing both the look and logic of my projects and look forward to learning more.

When I'm away from my computer, you can likely find me in the gym or biking on the Lakefront Trail.
